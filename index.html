About

xhBalck is a movement born in 2008 in Copenhagen with the focus on life, fun and freedom.

The festival has had its debut in 2010. Three years later the festival had to be shut down due financial reasons. Thanks to very kind sponsors and partnerships, we were able to resurrect and jump to a new era of xhBalck festival with a massively new budget.
